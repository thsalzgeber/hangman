Instructions

The game system will randomly select a word for you to guess. You'll see a series of blanks representing each letter of the chosen word. Guess a letter by clicking on the virtual keyboard. After each guess, the game will reveal whether the guessed letter is in the word. If correct, the letter(s) will appear in the corresponding blanks. If incorrect, the game will keep track of your incorrect guesses.

The game will prevent you from exceeding the maximum of six incorrect guesses.

You win if you correctly guess the word before making six incorrect guesses. You lose if you reach the maximum of six incorrect guesses, and the game reveals the correct word.

Enjoy your single-player Hangman game online!
